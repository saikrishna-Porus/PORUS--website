Porus Citizen Services
Private Limited

Building digital products that simplify access to information and services.

Porus Citizen Services Private Limited is a technology-focused company dedicated to creating digital solutions that improve accessibility, usability, and user experience. The company develops platforms that help people discover information, access services, and interact with technology in a simpler and more meaningful way.

Our Vision

To build accessible digital products that solve real-world problems through technology

  • Making it easier for people to find services and information they need
  • Building apps that are simple and easy to use for everyone
  • Supporting multiple Indian languages
  • Creating products that work reliably, even in low-connectivity areas
  • Helping everyday people get things done without needing any technical knowledge
Our Mission

Good technology should feel simple-not complicated.

At Porus, we believe technology should get out of the way and just work. When you pick up your phone and need something done, you shouldn't have to figure out how to use the app. We build with that in mind-always.
